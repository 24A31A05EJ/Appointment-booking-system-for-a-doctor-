Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to share my medical information on MediCare?

+

Yes, absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and HIPAA-compliant security measures to protect all your medical information. Your privacy is our top priority.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

+

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel appointments through your account dashboard up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. A notification will be sent to the doctor.

Do I need to create an account to book an appointment?

+

Yes, creating an account is required. This ensures secure access to your medical records and appointment history. Registration is quick and free.

What payment methods are accepted?

+

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, and insurance payments. Payment is secure and processed through our encrypted payment gateway.

Is there a mobile app available?

+

Yes, MediCare is available on both iOS and Android. Download the app from your respective app store for a better mobile experience.
